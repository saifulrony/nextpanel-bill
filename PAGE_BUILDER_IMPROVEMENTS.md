### Build a Page:

1. **Open Page Builder**
   - Go to `/admin/customization`
   - Click "Page Builder" tab
   - Opens in new tab

2. **Add Components**
   - Click or drag components from sidebar
   - Components appear in canvas

3. **Edit Components**
   - Click on any component

   - Edit content, colors, fonts, etc.
